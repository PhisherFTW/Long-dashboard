  • Focus on putting yourself in a position to trade teammates or have them trade you instead of trying to go solo
  • When holding a corner make sure to position your crosshair a fair distance from the corner and try to imagine what kind of peek the enemy will most likely do against you.
  • Always point your crosshair at the next most likely position for an enemy to peek you from even if you are falling back and think they probably won't peek
  • Try to take advantage of each sides advantages like T side: Weaponry Advantage / Peekers advantage and CT sides: Positional advantage / Holding advantage
  • Different molly lineup for top banana which is more effective and safer
  • When playing against pistols its important to try maintain and keep your distance because the closer you get to them the more dangeorus they become.

  • When playing with the Famas try playing it like its an upgraded SMG as you will have alot more success like that because the Famas is by far the weakest rifle and trying to take long range fights will be difficult
